Portal Overview

Online-based Soil Health Report system for scheme administration and district-level report generation.

This prototype includes one Scheme Administrator account that can create and manage district user accounts, inspect all district data, and monitor generated Soil Health Reports. District users can log in, enter soil testing data, and generate recommendation-ready Soil Health Reports.

About Us

Soil Health Report Team Center

The Soil Health Report Team Center under the Department of Soil & Water Conservation, Kohima, Nagaland supports scientific soil testing, district coordination, and data-driven advisory for farmers through the research and training programme.

Research Scheme

Research and Field Support

The research scheme helps collect soil data, test key parameters, generate Soil Health Reports, and provide practical recommendations for balanced nutrient use, soil conservation, and better productivity.

Benefits

Why This Programme Matters

  • Supports accurate fertilizer and nutrient planning.
  • Improves soil health monitoring across districts.
  • Helps generate recommendation-based Soil Health Reports.
  • Provides programme-wide inspection for the scheme administrator.

Account Rules

Portal Access Model

  • One Scheme Administrator controls all district accounts.
  • Administrator can inspect all Soil Health Report records.
  • District users can only create and inspect their district records.
  • All saved records stay in browser local storage for this research and training portal.
