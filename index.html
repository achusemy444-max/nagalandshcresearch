Portal Overview

Online-based Soil Health Card system for scheme administration and district-level card generation.

This prototype includes one Scheme Administrator account that can create and manage district user accounts, inspect all district data, and monitor generated Soil Health Cards. District users can log in, enter soil testing data, and generate recommendation-ready Soil Health Cards.

About Us

Soil Health Card Team Center

The Soil Health Card Team Center under the Soil and Water Conservation Department, Kohima, Nagaland supports scientific soil testing, district coordination, and data-driven advisory for farmers through the research programme.

SHC Scheme

Research and Field Support

The SHC Scheme helps collect soil data, test key parameters, generate Soil Health Cards, and provide practical recommendations for balanced nutrient use, soil conservation, and better productivity.

Benefits

Why This Programme Matters

  • Supports accurate fertilizer and nutrient planning.
  • Improves soil health monitoring across districts.
  • Helps generate recommendation-based Soil Health Cards.
  • Provides programme-wide inspection for the scheme administrator.

Account Rules

Portal Access Model

  • One Scheme Administrator controls all district accounts.
  • Administrator can inspect all Soil Health Card records.
  • District users can only create and inspect their district records.
  • All saved records stay in browser local storage for this demo.
